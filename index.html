Who We Are

About Us

Futuring from the Ground Up

Anaadi Ecofutures is a research-driven organisation dedicated to building sustainable energy futures for farming communities and rural populations across India. We combine rigorous scientific inquiry with ground-level action — running our own organic farm, advising institutions, and developing proprietary green technologies — all centred on one powerful, underutilised resource: govar.

Cow dung and bio-waste have sustained Indian agriculture for millennia. We believe they can sustain its energy future too. Our work transforms these materials into biofuels, construction materials, packaging, and more — creating new, continuous revenue streams for the farmers who need them most.

We are not just imagining a better world. We are building it, one farm at a time.

Innovation

Research

Rethinking the Circular Farm

Our research focuses on developing and refining circular energy systems built around cow dung and bio-waste — materials that are abundant, renewable, and currently under utilised.

🔥

Govar-Based Biofuels

We are working on frugal innovation and business models to convert govar (cow dung) into high-value biofuels — including cooking and industrial gas, aviation fuel, and rocket fuel. These fuels have the potential to offer farmers a consistent, alternative revenue stream, reducing dependence on monsoons and market volatility alike.

🧱

Bio-Construction Materials

Using a proprietary blend of cow dung, soil, and other natural ingredients, we are developing sustainable construction materials that are low-cost, locally sourced, and carbon-negative. This includes a custom-designed system of interlocking bricks made from govar — durable, affordable, and entirely biodegradable.

📦

Bio-Cardboard from Cow Dung

We are pioneering the development of bio-cardboard derived from cow dung — a sustainable alternative to conventional packaging materials for non-food grade applications. As global pressure mounts on plastic and virgin paper packaging, govar-based cardboard offers a circular, scalable solution.

Living Proof

Our Farm

Proof of Concept. Acres of Evidence.

5+ Tonnes per acre — organic rice yield

Our organic farm is where research meets reality.

Using only a blend of cow dung and gomutra (cow urine) as fertilisers — with no synthetic chemicals, no pesticides — we have achieved rice yields of over 5 tonnes per acre. That number matters. It demonstrates, conclusively, that organic and productive are not opposing ideas.

Our farm serves as a living model: a replicable, scalable demonstration of what circular, cow-based agriculture can look like in practice — and what it can produce.

We also sell rice harvested from our farm directly to consumers who value knowing exactly where their food comes from and how it was grown.

The Resource

Why Govar?

The Most Underestimated Resource in Indian Agriculture

300M+ Cattle in India

India is home to over 300 million cattle. Every day, they produce an extraordinary volume of dung — the vast majority of which is either wasted or used in ways that barely scratch the surface of its potential.

Govar is carbon-sequestering. It is a natural fertiliser, a construction material, a fuel source, a packaging substrate. Processed thoughtfully, it can power homes, build walls, fertilise fields, and package goods.

It is not a new idea. It is an ancient one — reimagined with the rigour and ambition it deserves.

We are building the science, the infrastructure, and the market around it.
